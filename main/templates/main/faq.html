FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW DO I SIGN INTO MY ACCOUNT?

Click on the “Account” option on the menu and enter you sign in details that you used to create an account with us, then you will be signed into your account.

HOW CAN I CANCEL MY ORDER?

Go to your order menu and you will see the option for canceling orders.

CAN YOU SHIP INTERNATIONAL?

YES we can! We use DHL Express as a mode of shipping. for it is faster and safer. We are however flexible to use a service that you prefer, if it is possible we will do it for you.

TRACK YOUR ORDER.

After you have made your order, you will immediately get a confirmation message from us. From your portal, you will be able to see the stage which your order is at. For international orders, we will also provide tracking number for you to see where the order is.

MODE OF PAYMENT?

Cash on delivery-ONLY applicable within Nairobi, for pick-ups and deliveries.

Mpesa money transfer- We encourage the use of M-pesa as it also acts as a receipt.

Credit card-Applicable for all shoppers and especially those out of Kenya. PayPal- This is a PREFERRED mode of payment especially for our international shoppers.

WHERE CAN I FIND MORE HELP TOPICS?

If you haven’t found help kindly send us a direct message and we will reply to you within hours.

HOW DO I SEE MORE PRODUCT DETAILS?

Click on the desired product then scroll down and you will be able to see more details of the products in the description section.

DO YOU SELL YOUR PRODUCTS IN WHOLESALE FOR RESELL?

Yes we do. We accept wholesale orders which will be processed after a given period . Kindly contact us on your preferred order.

PERSONAL INFORMATION SECURITY

Your personal information will not be shared to third party for personal gains or use. Keep your account secure and ensure your login details are kept safe and don’t share with other people.

I HAVE RECEIVED A FAULTY ITEM.

After receiving your order and in an unfortunate situation it is broken, kindly take a photo and send it to us then we can know how to settle the matter.Kindly read our shipping and returns policy https://www.curiokenya.com/shipping/

CAN I RETURN A GIFT VOUCHER?

Unfortunately you cannot return a gift voucher.

HOW CAN I KNOW THE PRICE IN KENYA SHILLINGS?

The prices show up in US Dollars, the conversion rate is Ksh. 100. That is USD 1 = Ksh 100. Therefore is a products is USD 5.00 * 100 = Ksh500
